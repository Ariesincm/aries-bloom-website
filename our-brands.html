Our Brands

Four Members of One Luxury Family.

Each brand stands on its own — its own name, team, and clientele. Together, they follow a woman from becoming, to dressing, to mothering, to celebrating.

01 · Beauty & Self-Expression

BibisGlambar

BibisGlambar is a luxury beauty and hairstyling studio built around Timeless Soft Glam — refined, editorial makeup and styling for women who want to look flawless in photographs and feel even better in person. From bridal beauty to everyday glam, every session is a premium, one-on-one experience.

Mission

To help every woman feel confident, seen, and beautiful — never by hiding who she is, but by drawing her natural beauty forward.

Signature Offering

Bridal glam trials and day-of styling — the service that started it all, still perfected one client at a time.

Visit BibisGlambar
02 · Fashion & Couture

Sasy Couture

The women BibisGlambar styled kept asking the same question: where do I find a dress that actually fits me? Sasy Couture was built to answer it — a luxury fashion house dedicated to timeless elegance and exceptional craftsmanship.

Mission

To prove that fashion can tell a story and empower the woman wearing it, one handcrafted garment at a time.

Signature Offering

Made-to-order bridal and occasion couture, fitted over multiple sessions to the body alone.

Visit Sasy Couture
03 · Motherhood & Wellness

Baddie Mums Club

As beauty and couture clients became mothers, a new question emerged — where did the rest of me go? Baddie Mums Club exists for exactly that woman: a wellness and confidence community built around beauty, fitness, self-care, and connection.

Mission

To help mothers rediscover themselves, without asking them to set their identity aside.

Signature Offering

Small-group wellness circles pairing movement, mindset, and honest conversation.

Visit Baddie Mums Club
04 · Celebration & Events

BB Soirée & Events

The milestones our clients were already planning around us — baby showers, bridal moments, birthdays that mattered — became a brand of their own. BB Soirée & Events is a luxury event house dedicated to the art of celebration.

Mission

To make sure life's most meaningful moments are planned and honored the way they deserve to be.

Signature Offering

Full-service celebration planning — design, decor, and day-of management in one hand.

Visit BB Soirée & Events
