The Approach

Made to order, made to move.

Sasy Couture began as an answer to a simple, repeated frustration: ready-to-wear rarely fits a real body the way it should. Every piece starts with a fitting, not a size chart.

We work in silk, structured cottons and hand-finished detailing, with a pattern drafted to your measurements alone.
