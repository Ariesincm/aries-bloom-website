The Approach

A room that doesn't ask you to disappear.

Baddie Mums Club began as an off-hand comment from clients who missed feeling like themselves. It grew into a standing community — part wellness practice, part honest conversation.

We meet in person and online, in circles built for mothers at every stage, from newborn fog to teenage independence.
