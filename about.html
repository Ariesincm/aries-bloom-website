About Aries Bloom Inc.

A root system for four brands.

We didn't set out to build a portfolio of companies. We set out to take care of one woman well — and kept discovering new seasons of her life that deserved the same care.

Our Origin

Started in a single chair.

Aries Bloom Inc. traces back to one beauty chair, one client at a time, and a founder who noticed that the women she styled needed more than a good blowout — they needed garments that fit, a community that understood the exhaustion of early motherhood, and someone to handle the details of the days worth celebrating.

What began as BibisGlambar became the root of a house. Each new brand grew from a real, repeated request from women already in the room.

Our Mission

To meet a woman in every season, and leave her more herself than we found her.

Our Story

How the house grew.

Chapter One

BibisGlambar opens its doors

A single studio built on hair, makeup and styling — and a philosophy that beauty work should leave a woman feeling more like herself, not less.

Chapter Two

Sasy Couture is founded

Clients kept asking where to find garments that actually fit the body in the chair. Sasy Couture began as a made-to-order answer.

Chapter Three

Baddie Mums Club forms

A wellness and confidence community, built for the clients who became mothers and needed a room that didn't ask them to disappear.

Chapter Four

BB Soirée & Events launches

Baby showers, bridal moments, milestone birthdays — the celebrations our clients were already planning around us became a brand of their own.

Today

Aries Bloom Inc. is formed

The four brands are formally united under one parent company — sharing a standard of care while keeping their own names, teams and identities.
