About Aries Bloom Inc.

Where Vision Became a Legacy

Aries Bloom Inc. is the story of one woman's belief that beauty, fashion, wellness, and celebration were never meant to be separate industries. They were always meant to be one continuous act of care.

Our Story

One Vision. Four Distinct Brands.

Aries Bloom Inc. is more than a company — it is a home. A home for beauty, for fashion, for wellness, and for the unforgettable moments that mark a life well lived. Beneath one name, four brands built with care and conviction come together: BibisGlambar, Sasy Couture, Baddie Mums Club, and BB Soirée & Events.

Each brand carries its own voice, its own craft, its own community — yet all four are guided by a single purpose: to help people see themselves clearly, wear their confidence openly, and celebrate the milestones that deserve to be remembered.

The Beginning

Our Journey

The Beginning

The vision takes shape

The idea for a luxury lifestyle house begins — one company built to carry beauty, fashion, wellness, and celebration under a single standard of care, rather than as separate, unrelated ventures.

Chapter One

BibisGlambar launches

The first brand opens its doors in Atlanta, delivering premium beauty experiences built on one enduring mission: to help every woman feel confident, seen, and beautiful.

Chapter Two

Sasy Couture is founded

Luxury fashion is introduced, expanding the client experience with made-to-order garments built for the body in front of us, not a size chart.

Chapter Three

Baddie Mums Club forms

A wellness and confidence community is created for mothers and women, built around a room that doesn't ask anyone to set their identity aside.

Chapter Four

BB Soirée & Events launches

Luxury event planning is added to the family, created to celebrate life's milestones with the same care and precision found across every brand.

Today

Aries Bloom Inc.

The four brands unite under one parent company, sharing a single vision while maintaining their own identities, teams, and expertise.

Meet the Founder

Bibi J.K.

Bibi J.K. is the Founder and Creative Visionary behind Aries Bloom Inc. Her story starts in Lagos, Nigeria, where, as a young girl, she found her calling in the smallest of gestures — styling her younger sister's hair, experimenting with makeup, sewing small pieces of clothing by hand. What began as childhood curiosity became a lifelong devotion.

Since 2013, Bibi has built her career one discipline at a time — first through Haminahk and HMK Services LLC, and later through the founding of BibisGlambar in Atlanta, where her early instinct for beauty finally found a permanent home. With each new venture, her vision expanded, always guided by the same conviction: that everyone deserves to feel confident, beautiful, valued, and empowered.

That conviction eventually grew beyond a single brand. It became Aries Bloom Inc. — a company built to carry BibisGlambar, Sasy Couture, Baddie Mums Club, and BB Soirée & Events forward together, united by one shared standard of excellence, creativity, and purpose.

More than a decade of hands-on experience across beauty, fashion, and entrepreneurship — from her earliest work in Lagos to building a multi-brand company in Atlanta — she leads Aries Bloom Inc. with the same instinct that started it all: an eye for beauty, a devotion to craft, and an unwavering belief that every person deserves to feel seen. Under her direction, the company continues to grow as a family of brands united by purpose, not simply a portfolio of businesses.

"I want every person who experiences an Aries Bloom brand to leave feeling confident, valued, and inspired — still authentically themselves, only elevated."

— Bibi J.K., Founder
Our Philosophy

The Principles That Define Us

I.

Quiet Confidence

We favor restraint over spectacle — a woman who is sure of herself doesn't need to raise her voice.

II.

Craft Over Trend

Every brand is built on real technique — tailoring, styling, planning — not fleeting aesthetics.

III.

Every Season Honoured

IV.

Community, Not Audience
