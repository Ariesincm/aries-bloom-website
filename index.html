About Aries Bloom Inc.

One House. Four Brands. One Purpose.

Aries Bloom Inc. is more than a company — it is a home. A home for beauty, for fashion, for wellness, and for the unforgettable moments that mark a life well lived. Beneath one name, four brands built with care and conviction come together: BibisGlambar, Sasy Couture, Baddie Mums Club, and BB Soirée & Events.

Each brand carries its own voice, its own craft, its own community — yet all four are guided by a single purpose: to help people see themselves clearly, wear their confidence openly, and celebrate the milestones that deserve to be remembered. Whether it is a hairstyle that finally feels like you, a gown made for your body alone, a wellness circle that welcomes you exactly as you are, or an event designed around the story you want to tell, every experience begins with the same question — how do we help you become more fully yourself?

We built Aries Bloom Inc. on the belief that beauty and empowerment are not separate pursuits. They are the same pursuit, worn differently by each person who becomes part of our story. That is the thread that runs through everything we do, and the promise we make to every client, every member, and every guest who walks through our doors.

Beauty & Self-Expression

BibisGlambar

BibisGlambar began the way most beautiful things do — quietly, with a steady hand and a labor of love. Long before it was a brand, it was a girl in Lagos, Nigeria, styling her younger sister's hair and discovering, session by session, that beauty work is really about attention — about seeing someone fully. Years of devoted practice later, that instinct became BibisGlambar, founded in Atlanta with one enduring mission: to help every woman feel confident, seen, and beautiful — never by hiding who she is, but by drawing her natural beauty forward.

Meet the Founder

A Story Rooted in Purpose

Biography

Bibi J.K. is the Founder and Creative Visionary behind Aries Bloom Inc. Her story starts in Lagos, Nigeria, where, as a young girl, she found her calling in the smallest of gestures — styling her younger sister's hair, experimenting with makeup, sewing small pieces of clothing by hand. What began as childhood curiosity became a lifelong devotion.

Since 2013, Bibi has built her career one discipline at a time — first through Haminahk and HMK Services LLC, and later through the founding of BibisGlambar in Atlanta, where her early instinct for beauty finally found a permanent home. With each new venture, her vision expanded, always guided by the same conviction: that everyone deserves to feel confident, beautiful, valued, and empowered.

That conviction eventually grew beyond a single brand. It became Aries Bloom Inc. — a company built to carry BibisGlambar, Sasy Couture, Baddie Mums Club, and BB Soirée & Events forward together, united by one shared standard of excellence, creativity, and purpose.

Experience

More than a decade of hands-on experience across beauty, fashion, and entrepreneurship — from her earliest work in Lagos to building a multi-brand company in Atlanta.

Leadership Vision

Bibi leads Aries Bloom Inc. with the same instinct that started it all — an eye for beauty, a devotion to craft, and an unwavering belief that every person deserves to feel seen. Under her direction, the company continues to grow as a family of brands united by purpose, not simply a portfolio of businesses.

"I want every person who experiences an Aries Bloom brand to leave feeling confident, valued, and inspired — still authentically themselves, only elevated."

— Bibi J.K., Founder
