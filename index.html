About Aries Bloom Inc.

One House. Four Brands. One Purpose.

Aries Bloom Inc. is more than a company — it is a home. A home for beauty, for fashion, for wellness, and for the unforgettable moments that mark a life well lived. Beneath one name, four brands built with care and conviction come together: BibisGlambar, Sasy Couture, Baddie Mums Club, and BB Soirée & Events.

Each brand carries its own voice, its own craft, its own community — yet all four are guided by a single purpose: to help people see themselves clearly, wear their confidence openly, and celebrate the milestones that deserve to be remembered. Whether it is a hairstyle that finally feels like you, a gown made for your body alone, a wellness circle that welcomes you exactly as you are, or an event designed around the story you want to tell, every experience begins with the same question — how do we help you become more fully yourself?

We built Aries Bloom Inc. on the belief that beauty and empowerment are not separate pursuits. They are the same pursuit, worn differently by each person who becomes part of our story. That is the thread that runs through everything we do, and the promise we make to every client, every member, and every guest who walks through our doors.

Why Choose Aries Bloom

The Aries Bloom Difference

Every brand under the Aries Bloom name is built on the same foundation — four commitments that shape every service, every garment, every gathering, and every experience we create.

Excellence

We hold every detail — from a single hair strand to a wedding-day timeline — to a standard that never asks "is this good enough," only "is this our best." Excellence is not a claim we make; it is a discipline we practice.

Creativity

Innovation and thoughtful design guide everything we build. We treat beauty, fashion, wellness, and celebration as creative disciplines, never formulas — because every person we serve deserves something considered, original, and truly their own.

Empowerment

Every brand under the Aries Bloom name exists to help someone feel more like themselves, not less. Confidence is not something we sell — it is the outcome we design for, in every chair, fitting room, wellness circle, and event we create.

Meaningful Experiences

We are not in the business of transactions. A hairstyle, a gown, a wellness gathering, a celebration — these become memories, and memories are what we are truly building. Every Aries Bloom brand is committed to creating moments people carry with them long after they end.
