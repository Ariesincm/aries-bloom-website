Life Inside Aries Bloom

What It Actually Feels Like to Work Here

Working inside Aries Bloom Inc. means working inside four distinct crafts under one shared standard. A stylist at BibisGlambar, a patternmaker at Sasy Couture, a facilitator at Baddie Mums Club, a planner at BB Soirée & Events — different tools, different rooms, the same conviction that the person in front of you deserves your full attention.

We hire for real skill first, and we protect the space for that skill to grow. Many of our team members have moved between brands entirely as their own interests evolved — because we built one company, not four silos.

Our Culture

Six Things We Protect No Matter How Big We Grow

Creativity

We treat beauty, fashion, wellness, and celebration as creative disciplines. Your ideas are expected, not just tolerated.

Excellence

We hold our work to a standard that never asks "is this good enough" — only "is this our best."

Craftsmanship

We hire for real technical skill — styling, patternmaking, planning, facilitation — and invest in it continuously.

Collaboration

Four brands, one operations team, one standard of care. We build across brand lines, not around them.

Growth

Many team members have moved between brands as their interests evolved. We plan for that, not around it.

Community

Every role, from styling to admin, exists to make a client — and a colleague — feel taken care of.
